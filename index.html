Our History

From the moment we poured our first cup, Narendra's Coffee shop has been about more than just coffee.It's about creating awarm, inviting space where friends gather, ideas percolate, and memories are made

At Narendra's Coffee shop, our history is as rich as the aroma of our freshly brewed coffee. Since 2020, we've been brewing up happiness, fostering connections, and building a legacy that's as robust as our espresso

For over 4 years, we've been dedicated to crafting exceptional coffee experiences. From humble beginnings to our bustling café today, our journey is steeped in a love for coffee and community
